~~~Profile of Fei MISS-A~~~

Fei was born Wang Feifei on April 27, 1987 in Haikou, Hainan, China. She studied dance at a school in Guangzhou. She later attended Seoul Institute of the Arts, alongside Miss A member Jia. Prior to debuting in South Korea, Fei appeared on the Zhejiang TV audition program, The More She Dances the Prettier She Is. She was a member of the project group, Sisters, the Chinese version of the Wonder Girls, but joined the South Korean girl group Miss A after the Sisters project was cancelled. In March 2010, Fei, with fellow members Jia and Suzy, formed the group Miss A. The group was later joined by a fourth member, Min. Fei made her acting debut with cameo appearances in the KBS drama Dream High in 2011 and its sequel in 2012.

In 2013, she appeared on the South Korean variety shows, MasterChef Korea Celebrity and Dancing with the Stars 3, in which she won second place and first place, respectively. She appeared on the Chinese reality show, If You Love, in 2014. That same year, she made a cameo appearance in the SBS drama Temptation, and contributed the song, "One Summer Night," featuring 2AM member Jo Kwon, to the soundtrack.

In 2015, Fei was confirmed to star in the web drama Swan, alongside Nam Gyu-ri and Cho Min Sung. She was also confirmed to star in the Chinese movie, Select Game. Fei made her solo singing debut in 2016 with the single, "Fantasy," which Billboard magazine critics named one of the 20 best K-pop songs of 2016. On January 23, 2019, Fei confirmed that she had parted ways with JYP Entertainment in late 2018 after her contract expired. She signed with Huayi Brothers in order to pursue her solo career in China.
