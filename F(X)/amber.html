~~~Profile of AMBER F(X)~~~

Liu was born on September 18, 1992, in Los Angeles, California. Her parents are from Taiwan and her father has Teochew ancestry. She has one older sister named Jackie. She attended El Camino Real High School in Los Angeles. Liu was cast from SM Entertainment's Los Angeles global audition in 2008, she debuted as a member of the South Korean girl group f(x) in September 2009. She worked with fellow group mates Luna and Krystal for the soundtrack of KBS drama God of Study, in a song called "Spread its Wings". In June 2010, she was featured in the lead track of Taiwanese singer, Danson Tang's third Mandarin studio album, The First Second. Liu has also made a career on television. From October 2011 to April 2012, she was part of the reality-variety show, Invincible Youth. She had to leave the show to prepare for her group's debut in Japan. She has hosted MBC's music show Show Champion with T-ara's Eunjung for a year, both of them stepped down as hosts on December 18, 2013.

On June 6, 2014, she became a host of the KBS show, A Song for You with Kangin of Super Junior and Yook Sungjae of BTOB. She was also a host of We Got Married Global Edition. Liu officially debuted as a solo artist, releasing her debut EP, Beautiful, on February 13, 2015. The title track, "Shake That Brass", featured Girls' Generation's Taeyeon. As a part of SM Station project, Liu's solo single, "Borders", was released in March 2016. She participated in composing, lyrics writing and arrangement of the song. Liu released digital single "On My Own" on May 18, 2016.

On May 25, she released "Need to Feel Needed". In July 2018, she signed with Steel Wool Entertainment and was also registered as her full name, Amber Liu. The agency represents and promotes her in the United States through singing, acting, as well as directing videos. On September 1, 2019, Liu announced her departure from SM Entertainment. Five days later, she confirmed that she would continue forward with her Steel Wool Entertainment contract, and would release an album that she previously prepared with the label.
