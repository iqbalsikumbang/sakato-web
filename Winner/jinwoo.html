~~~Profile of Jinwoo (Jinu) Winner~~~

Kim Jin-woo (born September 26, 1991) is a South Korean singer and actor. He is the vocal of the South Korean boy group Winner, formed in 2013 by YG Entertainment through the survival show Win: Who Is Next. Jinwoo was born on September 26, 1991 in Imja-do, Sinan County, South Jeolla. He was scouted by Seungri of K-pop boy band Big Bang, who was impressed by his dancing and hence bringing him to YG Entertainment. Following the scouting, he became a trainee at YG Entertainment in 2010. He was a backup dancer at the 2011 YG Family Concert, alongside fellow member Taehyun. 2013â€“14: Win: Who Is Next and debut. In 2013, Jinwoo made his first TV Show appearance on Mnet survival program Win: Who Is Next as a trainee under the group Team A. After 100 days of airing, the winner was revealed to be Team A hence proceeding to debut preparations following the end of the show. In August 2014, Jinwoo made his debut in the boy band Winner, along with fellow members Nam Tae-hyun, Kang Seung-yoon, Mino and Lee Seung-hoon. In Winner, Jinwoo fulfils the position of vocal.
