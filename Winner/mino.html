~~~Profile of Mino Winner~~~

Song Min-ho better known by the stage name Mino, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, producer and member of boy band Winner, signed under YG Entertainment. He appeared in the survival program Show Me the Money 4 , where he finished as series runner-up. Alongside Bobby, he is part of the hip hop duo MOBB and released the four-track album The Mobb in September 2016. He currently appears as a cast member in the show New Journey to the West. Song Min-ho was born on March 30, 1993, in Yongin, Gyeonggi Province. He has a younger sister named Dan-ah, who was a member of New F.O. He graduated from Hanlim Multi Art School in 2011. He was also a member of the underground rapping scene, under the name Mino or Hugeboy Mino, collaborating with other underground rappers-turned-idols, namely Block B's Zico, Kyung and P.O, Phantom's Hanhae, Speed's Taewoon, and M.I.B's Sims.

In 2011, Mino debuted as a rapper in the group BoM, under Y2Y Contents Company, following disbandment two years later. While still active in the group, Mino made his first acting appearance in March 2012 through Channel A cable drama The Strongest K-POP Survival. In 2013, Mino was accepted into YG Entertainment through auditions. In the same year, he competed as part of the trainee group "Team A" in Mnet's reality-survival program Win: Who Is Next. Initially, he was the leader, but due to an injury part-way through the show, he was replaced as leader by Kang Seung-yoon. During the finale episode on October 25, 2013, it was announced that Team A had won the competition, and would be debuting as Winner.
