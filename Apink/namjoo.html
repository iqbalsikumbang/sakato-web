~~~Profile of Namjoo Apink~~~

Kim Nam Joo is the lead vocalist, dancer, and rapper of Apink. She was born on April 15, 1995, in Seoul. Has 165 cm of height and weighs 45 kg. She is the only child. Her role model is Lee Hyori, S.E.S, and FIN.K.L.
