~~~Profile of HEECHUL SUPER JUNIOR~~~

Kim Hee-chul was born in Wonju, Gangwon Province, on July 10, 1983. His family consists of his parents and an older sister, Kim Hee-jin. In 2004, Heechul graduated from Sangji Youngsoe College with a bachelor's degree in English tourism translation. In addition, in 2009, he graduated from Sangji University with a bachelor's degree in informatics through Bachelor's Degree Examination for Self-Education. He joined other trainees under SM Entertainment to form the group Super Junior 05, the first generation of Super Junior. His first acting role was in the second season of teen drama Sharp 2, co-starring labelmates Go Ara and Kibum, which was loved by young people in 2005, he played Baek Jin-woo. At that time, he gained popularity among female student fans with his unique appearance that made him seem like he popped out of genuine manga. Heechul also appeared as a radio DJ on SBS Power FM's Young Street Radio with Park Hee-von from November 2005 until mid-2006.

Kim Hee-chul carried out his mandatory military service from September 1, 2011 to August 30, 2013. He served as a public service worker for 23 months after undergoing four weeks of basic training at the Nonsan military camp in Chungcheongnam-do. At the time on his enlistment, he was a host in a variety show, Secret, as well as the host of a talk show, Radio Star. His role in Radio Star was taken over by fellow bandmate, Kyuhyun. In 2015, he became MC on entertainment programs such as Wednesday Food Talk, A Style For You, MAPS, Knowing Bros and Korean-Chinese program With You All The Way. Heechul’s debut solo digital single, called "Old Movie", was released on April 24, 2019. It subsequently topped the charts in seven different countries.
