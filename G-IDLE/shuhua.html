~~~Profile of Shuhua G-Idle~~~

Yeh Shu Hua is a Taiwanese singer under Cube Entertainment. She is the vocalist, visual and maknae of the South Korean girl group (G)I-DLE. K-Pop was the most popular song genre in Taiwan then. By chance, she saw HyunA sunbaenim's stage and become a fan instantly. She made the decision that she must become a singer one day. One day her friend said "Let's go to Cube's Audition". She went with them just out of curiosity but was then informed of being qualified. She then become trainee and debut with (G)I-DLE
