~~~Profile of Miyeon G-Idle~~~

Cho Mi Yeon the main vocalist of the South Korean girl group (G)I-DLE. She was born on January 31, 1997. Has 161 cm height and weighs 46 kg. Miyeon was influenced by her father who enjoyed music. She listened to various types of music since she was young. As she went to middle school, she went on her first audition by her friend's recommendation to challenge her dream. So, she became a trainee of YG Entertainment by 5 years but when her debut didn't happen, she left the company. She passed the audition on Cube and worked really hard whilst being a trainee. She became happy because she could now sing and dance as she had in the past. Now, she has finally debuted alongside the other (G)I-DLE members.
