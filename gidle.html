(G)I-dle Young Girls / Individual Girls; short for Girl-Idol), stylized as (G)I-DLE, is a multinational girl group based in South Korea and formed by Cube Entertainment in 2018. The group consists of six members: Miyeon, Minnie, Soojin, Soyeon, Yuqi, and Shuhua. The group debuted on May 2, 2018, with their first extended play (EP) titled I Am.

Predebut, Debut EP

Soyeon was a female trainee who represented Cube Entertainment on the survival show Produce 101, peaking at 10th place on the fifth episode but failing to become a member of the winning girl group, I.O.I. Soyeon also competed in the third season of the rap competition show Unpretty Rapstar, where she finished as the second runner-up. She later debuted as a soloist, releasing two digital singles: "Jelly" and "Idle Song". Miyeon had previously trained under YG Entertainment between 2010 and 2015. Soojin was a former trainee under DN Entertainment in 2015. She trained as a member of girl group Vivivida, but left before the group's debut. Minnie was featured in Line Friends' album Dance Party, which was released in November 2017. On April 5, Cube Entertainment revealed the name of their upcoming girl group to be "(G)I-dle". Cube Entertainment began revealing the members of (G)I-dle with the release of individual photos of Miyeon and Yuqi on April 8. Photos of Soojin and Shuhua were released on April 10, followed by Minnie and Soyeon on April 12. (G)I-dle released their debut EP I Am on May 2 with the lead single "Latata". The video for "Latata" exceeded 5.9 million views within the first week. The EP debuted at number 13 on the Gaon Album Chart issued on May 10, 2018. On August 6, (G)I-dle held a small concert and recorded a flash-mob performance of "Latata" in Times Square and Washington Square Park.

Hann, Hey Senor

On August 14, (G)I-dle's first digital single, "Hann (Alone)", was released. The music video was released alongside the single on August 14. Within 24 hours, the video surpassed 4.9 millions views on YouTube. "Hann" topped domestic music charts including Bugs, Genie and Olleh Music on August 16 and hits a new peak on Billboard's World Digital Song Sales chart where they debut at No. 2. They received their first music show win on Show Champion on August 29 for "Hann". (G)I-dle released their second EP, I Made, on February 26, 2019. The EP contains five songs, including the lead single "Senorita", written and composed by member Soyeon and Big Sancho. On 26 June, (G)I-dle released its second digital single,"Uh-Oh". The song ranked top fifty at number 22 on NetEase Cloud Music China for the first half of 2019 making them the only Kpop group to chart in. On July 31, 2019, (G)I-dle will debut in Japanese market with the release of extended play Latata.


Sleeper Hit

(G)I-dle subsequently participated in a reality girl group survival show created by Mnet, Queendom. In the first preliminary round, (G)I-dle finished first place after performing "Latata" reimagined with a concept of shamanism. The introduction of the performance, Minnie's Thai "enchantment", was well received and became a hot topic among both Korean and Thai fans. In the second preliminary round (G)I-dle reinterpreted 2ne1's Fire, finishing in last place. At the end of September, they held their first fan meeting, Welcome to the Neverland, at Yes24 Live Hall in Seoul.On October 25, (G)I-dle released "Lion" as part of Queendom Final Comeback EP."Lion" became a sleeper hit after gaining popularity on their stage concept of The Queen's Royal Welcome, re-entering the real-time charts of various music sites.On January 28, 2020, the group announced their first world tour, titled "I-Land: Who Am I", which would span 32 different cities around the world. Later, it was announced their commenced show in Bangkok was postponed in response to the coronavirus outbreak and to ensure the safety and health of the artist, fans, and staff.
