~~~Profile of S.COUPS SEVENTEEN~~~

S.Coups (에스쿱스) is a South Korean rapper under Pledis Entertainment. He is a leader of the boy group SEVENTEEN and a leader of Hip-Hop Team. His position are main rapper and sub-vocalist. He was born in Daegu, South Korea in August 8, 1995. He has an older brother (2 years older than him).
