~~~Profile of JOSHUA SEVENTEEN~~~

Joshua (조슈아) is a South Korean singer under Pledis Entertainment. He is a member of the boy group SEVENTEEN. He belongs to the vocal unit. His position is Lead Vocalist. He was born in Los Angeles, California, US in December 30, 1995. His parents are both Koreans. He’s an only child. He joined Pledis Entertainment and began his training in 2013. He was a trainee for 2 years and 2 months.
