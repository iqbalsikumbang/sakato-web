~~~Profile of JUN SEVENTEEN~~~

Jun (준) is a Chinese singer and dancer, currently based in South Korea. He is a member of boy group SEVENTEEN. He belongs to the Performance Unit. His positions are lead dance and sub-vocalist. He was born in Shenzhen, Guangdong China in 10 June 1996. He has a younger brother (10 years younger than Jun). He was a trainee for 2 years and 7 months. He says many Chinese students love kpop and that’s how he became curious about it and moved to Korea.
