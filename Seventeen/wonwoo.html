~~~Profile of WONWOO SEVENTEEN~~~

Wonwoo (원우) is a South Korean rapper under Pledis Entertainment. He is a member of the boy group SEVENTEEN. He belongs to the Hip-Hop Team. His positions are Lead Rapper and Sub-Vocalist. He was born in Changwon, Gyeongsangnam-do, South Korea in 17 July 1996. He has a little brother named Jeon Bohyuk.

~~~Fun Facts~~~

– He participated in Pledis auditions just for experience but passed the audition.
– He was a trainee for 4 years.
– He started off as a singer but people told him he had a good voice for rapping. Since then, he looked into the Hip Hop scene and got noticed as a rapper and became a part of the Hip Hop team.
