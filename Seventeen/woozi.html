~~~Profile of WOOZI SEVENTEEN~~~

Woozi (우지) is a South Korean singer and producer under Pledis Entertainment. He is a member of the boy group SEVENTEEN and a leader of Vocal Team. His position is Lead Vocalist. He was born in Busan, South Korea in 22 November 1996. He’s an only child.
