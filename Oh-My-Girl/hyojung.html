~~~Profile of HYOJUNG OH-MY-GIRL~~~

Hyojung (효정) is a South Korean singer under WM Entertainment. She is the leader of the girl group OH MY GIRL and its sub-unit OH MY GIRL BANHANA. She was born on July 28, 1994 in Yangyang, Gangwon, South Korea.
