~~~Profile of MARK GOT7~~~

Mark Tuan is an American singer, rapper, and dancer, who is part of the South Korean boy band 'Got7.' He has released several EPs with the band and has appeared in the web drama ‘Dream Knight.’ Most of his singles for the band are chartbusters. Mark, with his incredible skills and talent, has won numerous awards and has successfully built an impressive fan base. He is highly active on social media, where he has millions of fans. Mark was born Mark Yi-Eun Tuan, on September 4, 1993, in Los Angeles, California, US. He is the first son of his parents, Raymond and Dorine Tuan. Mark’s father is an "Instagrammer" and is very popular with his son’s fans. He is from Taiwan.

Mark has two sisters, Tammy and Grace, and a younger brother, Joey. His family often calls him “Little Rooster,” as according to the Chinese calendar, his birth year represents the rooster. Mark attended 'Arcadia High School' in California. He often scored well in mathematics and physics. He spent some of his childhood in Brazil and Paraguay. Mark has traveled extensively, and this has helped him learn several languages.

He can speak Korean, English, and Mandarin fluently. While in school, Mark was highly encouraged by his teachers to learn singing and to participate in music competitions. With a bit of initial disinterest, Mark finally agreed to audition. He auditioned for a South Korean talent agency named 'JYP Entertainment,' which changed his life forever. Mark's rendition of Eminem’s track 'When I'm Gone' impressed the judges so much that he was immediately included in the agency’s roster. Mark then moved to South Korea, where he received his formal singing lessons.
