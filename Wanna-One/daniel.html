~~~Profile of KANG DANIEL WANNA ONE~~~

Kang Daniel was born as Kang Eui-geon in Busan, South Korea as the only child of his family. Prior to appearing on Produce 101 Season 2, Daniel had trained for two years and one month. He initially started as a trainee under B2M Entertainment until management separation, and then became a trainee under MMO Entertainment. He is known for finishing first in the final episode and becoming the center of the project boy group Wanna One under YMC Entertainment. He also continued to promote with Wanna One, including as a part of Wanna One's unit "Triple Position" which won Best Unit Award at the 2018 Mnet Asian Music Awards.

Kang officially debuted as a solo artist on July 25 with the debut EP Color on Me. Its lead single "What Are You Up To" gave Kang his first ever solo music show win on KBS2's Music Bank on August 9. The "Kang Daniel Show" aired on Chuseok holiday with a total of four episodes released each day from September 11 to 14.

In 2020, Kang returned to scheduled activities with solo reality show Hello, Daniel which premiered on March 4. On March 24, he released his second EP, Cyan, as the first installment of his colors trilogy project. Its lead single "2U" gave Kang his first top ten hit in Korea.
