~~~Profile of SUNGWOON WANNA ONE~~~

Ha Sungwoon was trained at Alpha Music Academy, when he got accepted in the 8th Open Audition of JYP through his dancing audition. He later joined Star Crew Entertainment (formerly known as Ardor&Able) and debuted with Hotshot as main vocalist on October 29, 2014, with a digital single "Take a Shot". Ha Sungwoon and Noh Taehyun represented Ardor&Able Entertainment in the boy group reality survival show, Produce 101 Season 2 which aired on Mnet from April 7 until June 16, 2017. In the final concert that took place on July 1 and 2, 2017, at Olympic Hall in Seoul, Sungwoon managed to gain 790,302 votes and was announced as the final member of the project boy group Wanna One under YMC Entertainment. He was also in the sub-group Lean on Me with Hwang Min-hyun and Yoon Ji-sung, performing the song "Forever and a Day" produced by Nell.

During his time with Wanna One, Sungwoon was invited as a cast in several TV programs, such as the 8th episode of SBS variety show Master Key, Law of the Jungle Sabah, which aired from July 27 to September 21, 2018, and MBC singing competition program King of Masked Singer. The singer began his solo activity by announcing his first two-day fan-meeting, "My Moment". Sungwoon's first solo album, My Moment, was released on February 28, 2019, featuring the upbeat lead single "Bird". The singer held a showcase at Live Hall in Gwangjin-gu, Seoul on February 27.

He appointed as a guest MC for SBS Inkigayo on March 2 and on March 13, Sungwoon took the trophy on his first solo with the song "Bird" on Show Champion. On June 5th, Ha Sungwoon unveiled his self-composed song "Riding" featuring Dynamic Duo Gaeko. Following the tradition, "Riding" is a pre-release track from Sungwoon's second mini album BXXX. The singer held his very first solo concert 'Dive in Color' in Seoul's Jamsil Indoor Stadium.
