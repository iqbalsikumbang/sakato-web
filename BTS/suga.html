~~~Profile of Suga BTS~~~

Min Yoon-gi (Hangul: 민윤기), better known as Suga (stylised as SUGA) or Agust D was born on March 9, 1993, in Buk District, Daegu, South Korea. His family consists of his mother, father, and older brother. Before his debut with BTS, he was an underground rapper in a group called D-Town and went by the stage name of GLOSS. He produced a song called 518-062. Yoongi chose to become a rapper after listening to Ragga muffin in middle school. He was poor and had to sell his records for money and was often scammed.
