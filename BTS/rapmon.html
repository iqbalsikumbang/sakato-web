~~~Profile of Rap Monster BTS~~~

Kim Nam-Joon (Hangul: 김남준) better known as RM (changed from Rap Monster), is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, and record producer. He is the leader and the main rapper of the South Korean boy group BTS, and made his debut on June 13, 2013, as a member of BTS. He has released two solo mixtapes, his first one, 'RM', was released in 2015. Since then, he also released a mixtape called mono. in 2018. He has successfully made his mixtape on the charts with the power and emotional connection that comes from the music. RM was born on 12 September 1994 in Ilsan-gu, Goyang, South Korea. He grew up with his younger sister, Kim Geong Min in Seoul. He attended Global Cyber University and majored with honours in Broadcasting and Performing Art. He was among the top rankers in the national school exam and had a TOEIC score of over 900, which is commendable. He also has a very high IQ of 148.
