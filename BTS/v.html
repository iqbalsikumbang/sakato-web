~~~Profile of V BTS~~~

Kim Tae-hyung (김태형) is also known as Kim Taehyung or by his stage name V, he is a member of BTS (a.k.a Bangtan Sonyeondan/ Bulletproof Boy Scouts) under BigHit Entertainment. V made his debut on June 13, 2013, as a member of BTS. V was born December 30, 1995 (age 23) in Daegu, South Korea. He lived in Goyang and Daegu before moving to Seoul. As the oldest of 3, V has one younger sister (Kim Eon-jin) and one younger brother (Kim Jeong-gyu). He began his entertainment career at the Korean Arts High School in Seoul, graduating alongside group-mate Jimin (Park Ji-min) in 2014.
