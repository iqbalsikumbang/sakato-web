~~~Profile of Jimin BTS~~~

Park Ji-min (박지민) is also known by his stage name Jimin. He is a member of BTS (a.k.a Bulletproof Boy Scouts/Bangtan Sonyeondan) under BigHit Entertainment. Park Jimin was born in Busan, South Korea. He is the oldest child in his family. He has one younger brother whose name is Park Ji-Hyun. Jimin became interested in a singing career after watching one of Rain’s performances. After his dance teacher recommended an audition, he auditioned for Big Hit Entertainment. After moving to Seoul, he began attending Korea Art School with V, where they graduated in 2014
