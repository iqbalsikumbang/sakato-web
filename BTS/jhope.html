~~~Profile of J Hope BTS~~~

Jung Ho-Seok (Hangul: 정호석), better known as J-Hope, is the main dancer and rapper of South Korean group BTS (AKA; Bangtan Sonyeondan). J-Hope made his debut on June 13, 2013 as a member of BTS, a group formed by BigHit Entertainment, with their debut single album 2 Cool 4 Skool. J-Hope was born in Gwangju, South Korea, on February 18, 1994. He has an older sister named Jung Dawon, who owns a clothing line called Mejiwoo. Before his debut, J-Hope was part of the underground street dance team "NEURON", where he earned many prizes and performed frequently during promotions. In March 1, 2018, he dropped his first mixtape, 'Hope World'.
