~~~Profile of SinB GFriend~~~

SinB also known as Hwang Eun-bi is the main dancer, face, and center of GFriend. She was born in Cheongju, in June 3, 1999. Has one older brother. She trained at Big Hit Entertainment along with Eunha for 5 years. She attended the School of Performing Arts Seoul and graduated in February 2017 along with Umji.
