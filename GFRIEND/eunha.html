~~~Profile of Eunha GFriend~~~

Eunha or also known as Jung Eun-bi is member of GFriend in charge of lead vocalist. She was born on December 7, 1995, in Seoul, South Korea. She has an older brother and an older sister. Graduated from School of Performing Arts Seoul.

~~~FUN FACTS~~~

-She attended the School of Performing Arts Seoul along with her fellow group members Yuju, Yerin, SinB, and Umji, and graduated in 2016 with Yuju and Yeojong from Lovelyz
– She was diagnosed with langerhans cell histiocytosis (LCH) when she was seven years old, she has to have treatments and regular blood checks, and is successfully cured
– Eunha is a former trainee of BigHit. She only trained there for 1 year.
– Eunha and SinB were friends when they were in their childhood days.
– SinB and Eunha have the same real name, EunBi.
– She only trained for 2 months under Source Music and got selected as a member of GFRIEND.
– GFRIEND members nicknamed her “Circle” because of her round-shaped face.
– She likes to cook. Her specialty is cooking mixed noodles.

– She likes to watch cooking shows.
– She acted in the 2007 movie “Love and War”.
– Eunha is a fan of Twilight movies.
– Sowon stated that Eunha was very quiet and didn’t talk a lot when they were trainees. (Vlive)
– Eunha appeared in Yu Seungwoo’s “You’re Beautiful” MV.
– She began in 2015 in a duet song with Pro C titled “Han River at Night“.
– In 2016, she released a duet song with Park Kyung called “Inferiority Complex“, and it gained attention from the public. They were nominated for Best Collaboration and Song of The Year in MAMA 2016, Best Rap/Hip Hop in the Melon Music Awards 2016 and Digital Bonsang in the Golden Disk Award.
– In 2016 she also released a duet song with Hwang Chi Yeol titled “Firefly” and “Chemistry” with MC Mong.
– She also had projects with other girl group members such as OH MY GIRL’s YooAh; WJSN’s Cheng Xiao, Gugudan Nayoung; and MOMOLAND’s Nancy, called Sunny Girls and released a song title “Taxi” for SBS’s “Inkigayo Music Crush Part 2”.
– She also sing the Original Soundtrack for the drama Six Flying Dragons entitled “Don’t Come To Farewell”
– In 2017, She released a duet song with TEEN TOP’s Chunji entitled “Hold Your Hand“.
– She contributed to the Original Soundtrack for dramas such as Temperature of Love, with the song entitled Love-ing“.
– She aslo sung the OST for the drama The Packaged with Yoon Ddan Ddan entitled “You Look Nice Today“.
– She sang the last OST for Grand Chase for Kakao entitled “Hope” in 2017.
– In 2016, when she was promoting her single “Navillera”, she shocked everyone by cutting her hair -, and getting bangs.
– Now, she is comfortable with her short hair. People said that she looks more attractive, because her short hair emphasizes her large eyes and pale skin.
– Her role model is IU.
– Her stage name Eunha means “galaxy”.
– Eunha was the second member to participate in ‘King of Masked Singer’, as “Fresh Santorini”.
– Eunha’s ideal type is Jo Jung Suk & Crush.
