~~~Profile of Yerin GFriend~~~

Kim Ye-rin is member of GFriend in charge of vocal and visual. She was born on August 19, 1996, in Incheon, South Korea. Her father’s name is Jung Young Don and her mother’s name is Jung Hyun Nam. She has an older brother, eleven years older.
