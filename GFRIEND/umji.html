~~~Profile of Umji GFriend~~~

Umji also known as Kim Ye-won is the lead rapper of GFriend. She was born in Incheon, on August 19, 1999. Has one older brother and older sister. The CEO of Source Music saw her walking on the street and invited her to make an audition. She attended the Seoul School of Performing Arts and graduated in February 2017 along with SinB.
