~~~Profile of Sowon GFriend~~~

Sowon or also known as Kim So-jung is member of GFriend in charge of leader, vocal and rapper. She was born on December 7, 1995. The tallest member with 172 cm height. She graduated from Hanlim Multi Art School and majoring in Visual Media and Acting at Sungshin Women's University.

– Sowon mentioned in “Idol Room” ep. 34 that she is 172.8cm (5’8.3″) and her legs are 113cm (3’8″).
