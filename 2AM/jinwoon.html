~~~Profile of JINWOON 2AM~~~

Jinwoon was born on May 2, 1991 in Seoul, South Korea. He graduated high school from BaekAhm High School. He was originally eliminated from 2AM in the Hot Blood documentary, but Jinwoon eventually returned after one of the chosen members Daehun had to return to the States for personal reasons. Prior to debut, he was in a band called "No Comment". He plays guitar, drums, bass guitar, bongo and piano. In 2009, he made an appearance on the television series Family Needed. He was hosting M.net's M! Countdown along with bandmate Jo Kwon in 2010.On August 7, 2011, the music video of his first solo single, "You Walking Towards Me" was released by Big Hit Entertainment. Jinwoon wrote and composed the song. It also features mixing by engineer Michael Brauer. Jinwoon held his debut stage last month[when?] at the Jisan Valley Rock Festival, where he performed "You Walking Towards Me". On November 25, he held his first solo concert at Hongdae V Hall in Seoul.

Through his concert, Jinwoon will deviating from his 2AM image to show a new "rocker" side to his personality." On February 29, 2012 he released his OST song "We Are The B" for KBS's Dream High 2 together with Kang So-ra, Jr. and Kim Ji Soo. He was then featured in his friend Nicole Jung's solo track "Lost" in Kara Collection, both in Korean and Japanese. He was cast for KBS's Drama Dream High Season 2 in 2012 as one of the main character Jin Yoojin, who dreams of becoming a rock star that attends Kirin Art School. Jinwoon paired with Go Jun Hee in We Got Married in 2013. Jinwoon being MC Music Bank with Park Se Young since April 12, 2013 until he got car accident and promote Park Seo Joon to replace him. He was cast as Han Yeo Reum in TVN's drama Marriage Over Dating in 2014.
