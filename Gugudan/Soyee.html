~~~Profile of Soyee GUGUDAN~~~

Soyee (Jang So-jin - Birthname) is a South Korean singer under Jellyfish Entertainment. She is the main vocalist of the girl group Gugudan. She was born on November 21, 1996 in Gwangju, South Korea.

~~~Fun Facts~~~

– Education: Seoul Chongmok Elementary School, Shin Mok-Jung Middle School, Kyungbok Girls’ High School and Baekseok Arts University (same schools as Nayoung)
– Her nickname is ‘Joy Bear’.
– She was a trainee for over 5 years.
– She was a Cube Entertainment and Plan A Entertainment trainee.
– She is the best of the group playing videogames.
– She did a duet song called ‘Spring Rain’ with Youngjoon from Brown Eyed Soul.
– She appeared in Leo & Lyn’s MV “Blossom Tears” MV and VIXX’s “Chained Up” MV.
– She was supposed to be a member of the girl group A PINK from Plan A Entertainment (formerly A Cube Entertainment), which plan failed, so in the end she got to be a member of Gugudan
– She loves Jazz music.
– She did the narration for Seo In Guk’s Bomtanaba.
– On October 19, 2017 Soyee went on hiatus in order to fully recover from a shoulder injury.
– She’s back from hiatus starting “The Boots” promotions.
– She was born on the same day as Lusty‘s Junhui and 4TEN‘s Hyejin.
– Soyee’s ideal type: someone with a cute eye smile. She likes Maze Runner’s Ki Hong Lee.
