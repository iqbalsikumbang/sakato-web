~~~Profile of Sejeong GUGUDAN~~~

Sally (Liu Xie Ning - Birthname, Ryu Sa-jeo - Korean Name) is a South Korean singer under Jellyfish Entertainment. She is the lead dancer of the girl group Gugudan. She was born on October 23, 1996.

~~~Fun Facts~~~

– Her hometown is Guangdong, China.
– Education: Beijing Normal University, Beijing Contemporary Arts Academy
– Sally’s motto: My life….fighting! Live while smiling
– She loves to make everyone laugh by goofing and joking around.
– She also can make dimples that look like a cat’s moustache.
– She speaks Chinese and Korean.
– She was a former CF model in China.
– She appeared on VIXX “Chained Up” MV
– She likes to catch cockroaches.
– Her hobbies are cooking, eating and shopping.
– Sally has been cast in the Chinese drama ‘Hello, My Rival’.
– She’s friends with CLC‘s Elkie and Pristin‘s/IOI‘s Kyulkyung.
– Sally is a contestant on the 2020 season of Produce 101 China (Produce Camp).
– Sally’s ideal type: a rapper. Someone she respects. She likes G-Dragon.
