~~~Profile of MINA GUGUDAN~~~

Kang Mi-na was born in Icheon, and later moved to Jeju City. She graduated from School of Performing Arts Seoul in 2018. On April 1, 2016, the final line-up of South Korean girl group I.O.I was announced, made up of the top 11 poll-winners. Mina finished ninth overall, with 173,762 votes, becoming an official member of the girl group. On June 10, 2016, YMC Entertainment revealed that Mina would not be taking part in I.O.I's unit promotions, but would instead return to her agency to debut and promote with upcoming South Korean girl group, Gugudan.

In June 2018, a Gugudan subgroup named Gugudan Semina was formed with Mina and members Sejeong and Nayoung. They debuted with the release of their self-titled single album on July 10, 2018. Kang made her acting debut in the MBC romantic comedy series Children of the 20th Century in 2017, acting as the younger counterpart of Han Ye-seul. In March 2019, Kang was confirmed as part of the cast for Law of the Jungle in Thailand. In April 2019, Kang was cast in tvN drama Hotel del Luna as Kim Yuna.
