~~~Profile of Seulgi Red Velvet~~~

Kang Seul-gi born on February 10, 1994, in Ansan, Gyeonggi-do. She is the lead vocalist and main dancer of Red Velvet. Has one older brother. She became trainee since age of 13, for 7 years. Was part of S.M Rookies, SM Ent's pre-debut group.
