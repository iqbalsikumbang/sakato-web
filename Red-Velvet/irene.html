~~~Profile of Irene Red Velvet~~~

Bae Joo-hyun as her stage name Irene, is the leader, center, and main rapper of Red Velvet. She was born in March 29, 1991. Born and raised in Daegu, South Korea. Has younger sister. Made a successful audition to SM Ent. and trained 5 years before debut. Irene was part of S.M Ent's pre-debut group, S.M Rookies.
