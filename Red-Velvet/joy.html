~~~Profile of Joy Red Velvet~~~

Park Soo-young or Joy is the sub vocal, dancer, and lead rapper in Red Velvet. Joy was born as Park Soo-young in Jeju Island, South Korea and grew up in Dubong District, Seoul. Her family consists of her parents and two younger sisters, Park Ji-yeong and Min-ji. She auditioned and was cast by SM Entertainment at the S.M. Global Audition in Seoul in 2012. She trained under the agency for two years, before beginning her career under the name, Joy.
