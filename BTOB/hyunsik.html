~~~Profile of HYUNSIK BTOB~~~

Im Hyun-sik was born on March 7, 1992, in Seoul, South Korea. His father, Im Ji-hoon, is a South Korean folk singer. He studied Practical Music at Howon University with group-mate Lee Chang-sub. Hyunsik debuted as the lead vocalist Cube Entertainment's boy group BtoB on March 22, 2012, with the promotional single, "Insane". In September 2016, Hyunsik became part of BtoB's first unit group, BtoB Blue that consists of the group's vocalists. In 2019, Hyunsik make a solo comeback with self-titled EP in October. Rendez-vous is the first solo album released by Im in seven years since his debut.

He participated in writing, composing, and arranging all the songs in the album. Following his solo debut, he held his first solo concert Rendez-vous at Bluesquare iMarket Hall on November 2 and 3, 2019 which was sold out in four minutes. In 2020, Hyunsik appeared on KBS's Open Concert, performing "Dear Love" and two songs with his father. On January 31, 2020, Hyunsik released his first live album, Rendez-vous (Live) that features a live version of 5 songs from his first solo concert Rendez-vous.
