~~~Profile of ILHOON BTOB~~~

Jung Il-hoon was born on October 4, 1994 in Seoul, South Korea. He graduated from Hanlim Multi Art School and attended Korea College of Media Arts with acting course. He is the younger brother of singer Joo. Jung debuted as the main rapper of the boy group BtoB in 2012. The group made their official debut on Mnet's M! Countdown on March 21. Since 2012, Jung has been continuously appearing on variety show Weekly Idol as a special MC/ together with Apink's Bomi. In 2013, he was featured in 2YOON's Harvest Moon EP for the track "Nightmare", where he showed his impressive rapping skills. In 2015, Cube Entertainment announced Hyuna's lead single for A+ would feature Jung. The track was titled "Roll Deep" (Because I'm the Best). He also participated in the making of the album.

On February 12, 2018, it was announced that Jung would be releasing his first solo album the following March. The album, titled Big Wave, contained five tracks which feature, BTOB member, Hyunsik and Jinho of Pentagon. The lead single, "She's Gone", is a hip hop song which describes complicated feelings, Jung had previously performed the song at BTOB's 2017 BTOB Time - Our Concert. Jung participated in writing and composing the songs. On February 21, 2019, Jung dropped a digital single, "Spoiler", featuring solo artist Babylon.
