~~~Profile of SUNGJAE BTOB~~~

Yook Sungjae was born on May 2, 1995, in Yongin, Gyeonggi, South Korea. He has one older sister. Yook comes from a business family. His father is the CEO of an IT corporation that deals with semiconductors. He briefly studied for a month each in England and the Philippines. He attended Hanlim Multi Art School and graduated in 2014. He then attended Dongshin University, majoring in Practical Music. On August 10, 2010, he participated in the stage performance of "2010 Cube Stars Party". Yook entered the JYP 6th Audition Final Round before joining his current agency, Cube Entertainment. Yook made his debut as a vocalist of BtoB with "Insane" and "Imagine" on March 21, 2012.

In 2013, Yook Sungjae started his first ever acting debut in drama Monstar (TV series) as part of the supporting cast alongside his bandmates Lee Min-hyuk, Lee Chang-sub and Im Hyun-sik. In 2014, Yook appeared in the television series Reply 1994 as Sungjoon/Ssukssuk, Na-jung's little brother. He also participated in the program Hitmaker season 1, presented by South Korean producer duo Jeong Hyeong-don and Defconn. Yook achieved his breakthrough when he landed a leading role in the teen drama Who Are You: School 2015, alongside Kim So-hyun and Nam Joo-hyuk. In 2015, Yook joined King of Mask Singer, competing under the alias Tired Bumblebee, and received good feedback for his performance.

He was paired up with Red Velvet's Joy in the fourth season of reality show We Got Married. In September 2015, Yook became one of the trio main hosts Yook Sungjae-GOT7's Jackson Wang-and actress Kim Yoo-jung of the music-program Inkigayo and left the show in May 2016. In 2018, Yook held his first fan meeting, Paradise at TICC in Taiwan on February 11. In 2019, during BtoB's seventh anniversary Yook was chosen as their new leader. On December 19, 2019, Cube revealed their plans for a new music project 3x2=6, which aims for Yook to release two songs every month for three months starting December 2019.
