~~~Profile of MINHYUK BTOB~~~

Lee Minhyuk was born on November 29, 1990 in Seoul, South Korea. He graduated from Kwangsung High School and attended Dankook University, majored in Musical. He's been active as an underground music artist by the name Heota and was featured in Block B's Zico and Park Kyung's songs. Lee made his debut as a member boy-group BtoB in 2012. The group made their official debut on March 21, 2012 on M! Countdown.

In 2012, Lee was cast in a variety show, tvN's The Romantic & Idol 2. He also had multiple appearances since 2013 on Let's Go! Dream Team Season 2. In 2014, he received supporting roles in dramas, A New Leaf and the Idol Special of Love & War 2. Following his acting career, he joined the casts of Sweet, Savage Family in 2015. He also starred as a lead in the web-drama Nightmare Teacher. Lee returned under the name Heota as he featured as a rapper in Postmen's "I'm OK" that was released on July 14, 2015. Lee is set to make his silver screen debut in the upcoming historical action film, The Swordsman, as Gyeom Sa-bok. Lee is set to make his solo debut in Japan with his first Japanese EP, Summer Diary, that will be released on July 25 with a total of five tracks. In January 2019, Lee's Korean debut was announced with all self-composed album 'Hutazone'. He will hold his first solo concert, HUTAZONE: Two Night on 2 and 3 February 2019 at the Yes24 Live Hall in Gwangjin District, Seoul
