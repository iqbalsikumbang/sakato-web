~~~Profile of Chanwoo IKON~~~

Jung Chan-woo was born on January 26, 1998 in Yongin, Gyeonggi do, South Korea. He is the maknae and sub-vocalist of the South Korean boy group IKON. In 2006, he appeared in the music video for TVXQ's "Balloons", as the younger Max Changmin. In 2008, he appeared in Lost and Found and several subsequent drama series, beginning with The Great King Sejong. He is known for playing a younger Lee Minho in both Boys Over Flowers and The Heirs. He has made appearances on survival programs WIN: Who Is Next and Mix & Match in 2013 and 2014 respectively. Soon after, in 2014, YG Entertainment announced the members of Team B would be competing again in a second reality survival show, Mix & Match. The show resulted in the debut of all Team B members alongside Jung Chan-woo under the name iKon.
