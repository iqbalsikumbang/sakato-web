~~~Profile of Donghyuk IKON~~~

Kim Donghyuk (Korean: 김동혁), better known as Donghyuk (동혁) or DK (디케이), was born on January 3, 1997. He is the main dancer and vocalist of the South Korean boy group IKON. He has made appearances on survival programs WIN: Who Is Next and Mix & Match in 2013 and 2014 respectively. Soon after, in 2014, YG Entertainment announced the members of Team B would be competing again in a second reality survival show, Mix & Match. The show resulted in the debut of all Team B members alongside Jung Chan-woo under the name iKon.
