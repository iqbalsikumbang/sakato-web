~~~Profile of Kihyun Monsta-X~~~

Yoo Kihyun is a South Korean singer under Starship Entertainment. He is the main vocalist of the boy group Monsta-X. He was born on November 22, 1993 in Goyang, South Korea. In December 2014, Kihyun competed in Mnet's survival show "No.Mercy". Along with the six other trainees, they formed a hip-hop idol group Monsta X and debuted under Starship Entertainment on May 14, 2015. Kihyun appeared as a contestant on the King of Mask Singer known as "Tell Them I’m the Dragon King" on January 3, 2016.

He won the first round versus Apink's Namjoo known as "Good Daughter Shim Cheong". In May 2018, he recorded "Can't Breathe" for MBC's Partners for Justice with fellow Monsta X member Jooheon, while in June, he recorded "Love Virus" for TvN's drama What's Wrong with Secretary Kim together with WJSN's SeolA. In April 2020, Kihyun recorded another OST for KBS's Welcome (TV Series), titled "Again Spring" (다시, 봄)
