~~~Profile of Momo TWICE~~~

Momo was born on November 9, 1996, in Kyotanabe,Kyoto,Japan. She is the main dancer of Twice, Has 168 cm of height and weighs 46 kg. Her blood type is A (She recently knew it in Twice's Private Life). Her role model is Rain and Lee Hyo Ri. She learned how to dance since age 3 and go to a dance studio called "Stepout Dance Studio" in Osaka, Japan. She decided to become a singer when she watched the famous Japanese singer Amuro Namie perform. Her older sister is Hirai Hana, with her Momo auditioned at JYP Ent. but only Momo passed.

~~Journey Begins~~

Her parents were completely agains her career choice and moving to Korea. After countless attempts of persuading, they agreed, under the condition that she would come back if she doesn't succeed. Then she begin as a trainee in JYP since 2012. She felt guilty of her sister because she's the one who passed the audition. She appeared in MV Junho "Feel" & GOT7 "Stop Stop It" in 2014, Miss A "Only You", Wooyoung "R.O.S.E", and Heechul and Min Kung Hoon "Sweet Dream". After training for 3 years, then through survival program "Sixteen", she made her debut. She is the last member of Twice to be announced. Originally, Sixteen program only debut 7 members but CEO JYP, production team and staff thought they needed more members, so they add Momo and Tzuyu. In Momo's case, she was chosen because she shows the best dancing among other competitors, and she was unbeatable at dance. Earned her the title "Dancing Machine" in her group.
