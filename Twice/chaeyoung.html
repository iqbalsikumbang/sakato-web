~~~Profile of Chaeyoung TWICE~~~

Son Chae-young was born in April 23, 1999, in Seoul, South Korea. Has B blood type, 160cm tall, and weighs 48kg. She has one younger brother, Son Jeong Hoon.

~~Journey Begins~~

Chaeyoung join dance training when she was in sixth grade and took audition for JYP Entertainment when she was 13. She appeared in GOT7's "Stop Stop It" and Miss A's "Only You". In SIXTEEN she was famous for her rap and got praised by San E (famous rapper Korea). Then she become main rapper in TWICE. Her role model is Nicki Minaj, because she thinks that Nicki Minaj is a charismatic artist.
