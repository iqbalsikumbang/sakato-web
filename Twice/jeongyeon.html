~~~Profile of Jeongyeon TWICE~~~

Jeongyeon was born on November 1, 1996, in Suwon, Seoul. She was given the name Yoo Kyung-wan, she changed her name to Yoo Jeong-yeon because she was being teased as having a boy's name. Her zodiac sign is Scorpio. She has about 5'6" tall (169cm) and weighs 50 kg (about 110 lbs). She has two older sisters. One of them is South Korean actress, Gong Seung-yeon. Their father is chef Yoo Chang Joon, who specializes in Korean traditional cuisine

Jeongyeon is one of the lead vocalists of JYP's girlgroup called TWICE. She has impressive dancing and singing skills. This girl has a manly yet classy personality and appearance, brings the order to the group most of the time by being strict and discipline but also has a sense of humor,and therefore embrace herself to the other members of TWICE.

~~Journey Begins~~

Jeongyeon tried out the 6th Open Audition for JYP Entertainment, and became one of JYP trainess on March 1st in 2010. In 2013, she was chosen as a member of 6mix just like nayeon, however they never debut and group disband. She appeared in some MV such as Got7's "Girls Girls Girls", Miss A's "Only You", and J.Y.Park's "Fire". The through survival program "Sixteen" she made her debut with the other eight members and became TWICE.
