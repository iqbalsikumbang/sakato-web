~~~Profile of Nayeon TWICE~~~

Nayeon is famous as one of the lead vocalists in her group called TWICE. She has impressive dancing and singing skills. With her bright personality, brings the light to the group most of the time by being cheerful and loud also always tries to stay positive.

~~Journey Begins~~

Before all that spotlight she's got, her audition for JYP was not smooth. She was still studying at school and her parents were opposed to her. After she passed the audition and became a trainee in September 15, 2010, she practiced dancing, singing and performing so hard to reach for her dream. She then appeared in TV ads and Music Video, including Got7's "Girls Girls Girls", "Stop Stop It", and Miss A's "Only You". She also appeared in one drama called "Dream High" as herself in 2012.She was chosen as a member called 6mix in 2013. But she never made her debut and disbanded. She then took part in a survival program by JYP Entertainment "Sixteen" in May 2015 and become one of the nine survivor to debut as girlgroup "TWICE" More than five years she finished her life as a trainee and she finally made her debut as a member of TWICE.
