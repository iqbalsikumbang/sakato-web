~~~Profile of Dahyun TWICE~~~

Kim Dahyun was born in May 28, 1998, in Seongnam, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. She is 165cm tall and weighs 48kg. In middle school, Dahyun perform as an solo artists in teenage dance festival. She began to attract attention when she still in sixth grade with her eagle dance. Then became viral in Youtube in 2011.

~~Journey Begins~~

She join JYP audition in July 7, 2012, and officialy became a trainee. As a trainee, she participated in her fellow JYP artist's Music Video, Got7's "Stop Stop It". Then she was chosen as one of the trainee to join survival program "SIXTEEN". She successfully debut as a TWICE member in October 20, 2015.
