~~~Profile of Jihyo TWICE~~~

Jihyo was born in February 1, 1997 in Guri, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. Her birthname is Park Ji-soo, but eventually changed it to Park Ji-hyo. She is 162cm tall and weihgs 56kg. Has two younger sisters. She was often mistaken as a foreigner when she was younger because of her big eyes and thick crease on her eyelids.

~~Journey Begins~~

JYP Entertainment began to scout her when she was in the third year Elementary school, right after she got second place in a child actor Junior Naver competition. Her parents have been supportive since that. She became trainee in JYP for ten years, but she was initially trained as an actress, just like Dahyun. She trained with WG, 2pm, 2am, Miss A, Got7 and literally everyone in JYP. Before her debut with Twice, Jihyo featured in the music video for Miss A's "Only You". She was known as the face of Innisfree's teen line.
