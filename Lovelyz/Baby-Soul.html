~~~Profile of Baby Soul Lovelyz~~~

Lee Soojung better known as Baby Soul is a South Korean singer under Woollim Entertainment. She is the leader of the girl group Lovelyz. She was born on July 6, 1992 in Gwangju, South Korea.

~~~Fun Facts~~~

– She had been a trainee for around 6-7 years.
– Baby Soul dropped out of high school to focus on training and moved to Seoul.
– She debuted as a solo artist in 2011.
– She has released two singles: “No Better Than Strangers” (2011) and “She’s a Flirt” (2012)
– She collaborated on two INFINITE H’s songs: “Crying” and “Fly High”.
– Babysoul and Sujeong sang the OST “Clean” for the drama “Second to Last Love”.
– She danced with Dongwoo in INFINITE’s Man in Love Performance for Gayo Daejun.
– Her hobbies are reading, cooking, writing lyrics.
– She can play the piano.
– Baby Soul’s favorite color is pastel purple.
– Baby Soul’s favorite food is sashimi.
– Babysoul can’t do aegyo, she is very shy to do it.
– Baby Soul appeared in King of Masked Singer too as “Fondue Princess”.
– Baby Soul and Sujeong share a room. [Ilgan Sport’s DrunkDol Interview]
